## Design System

### Colors
- Primary Text: Slate 900 (almost black)
- Secondary Text: Slate 600 (medium gray)
- Borders: Slate 200-300 (light gray)
- Backgrounds: White, Slate 50 (off-white), Slate 900 (dark sections)
- Accent: Clean slate/charcoal (NO purple/indigo)

### Typography
- Headings: Light to medium weight, generous spacing
- Body: Regular weight, 1.5 line height
- Size scale: 5xl for H1, 3xl-4xl for H2, xl-2xl for H3

### Spacing
- Generous white space throughout
- Consistent padding (8px grid system)
- Clear visual hierarchy

### Components
- Clean bordered cards (no shadows)
- Minimal, elegant buttons
- Simple icon usage
- Professional, readable forms
